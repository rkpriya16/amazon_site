Packing Workflow

Step 1: Picking Items

Efficiently pick items from designated shelves using barcode scanners.

Step 2: Quality Check

Ensure all items are in good condition before packing.

Step 3: Packing the Items

Carefully pack the items into boxes with protective materials.

Step 4: Labeling

Label each package with shipping information for tracking.

Step 5: Shipping

Send the packages to the shipping area for delivery.
